Work

Peddlr

Citi Bike is great, but planning a trip can be frustrating. Peddlr users are able to plan trips by entering the departure and destination locations. The application shows them where the nearest stations are with bikes or stalls available.

Valence

Valence is a tool for creating, organizing, and visualizing playlists. Using D3, users are able to view dynamic representations of their playlists according to traits like danciness, energy, and acousticness.

JustWrite

No formatting. No fonts. No fuss. JustWrite is a pared-down writing tool that eliminates all the destractions of typical writing programs. An invisible autosave mechanism ensures that no work is ever lost.

About

In the spring of 2014, I completed my Doctorate in Communications and Media Studies from the University of Illinois. During my PhD I taught and researched topics in new media, technology studies, and cultural studies. I also spent a lot of time knitting.

All the knitting I did made me realize how much I like making things. When I finished my PhD I enrolled in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive bootcampto learn how to make other things not involving yarn or needles. Now I'm a fullstack web developer living in New York.
